About Me

A summary about myself


Jeremiah Bassey Asuquo is an enthusiastic, capable, detail-oriented, result driven and motivated electrical electronics engineer and computer science specialist who aims to leverage finely-honed interpersonal and time management skills to successfully develop, manage, and deploy applications with the view of achieving corporate goals and organization objectives.

His journey as a Web Developer started when he enrolled in a course of study offered by MITx, an online learning initiative of Massachusetts Institute of Technology through edX, and in the Google Africa Scholarship in partnership with Andela and Udacity afterwards. He has also received excellent training from Google Africa Scholarship in partnership with Andela and Pluralsight. He is now proficient in Front-end and Back-end web technologies, System and Network Administration, Database Management System.

He has a B.Sc. in Computer Science from University of Calabar, Calabar, Nigeria expected by March 2020 after studying Electrical Electronics Engineering briefly at Covenant University, Ota. He has also completed various professional trainings and received certificates in Frontend and Backend Development, Project Management, Digital Marketing, Computer Science and Programming from APTECH Computer Education, The Open University, Google, and Massachusetts Institute of Technology, USA.
